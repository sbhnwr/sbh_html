Planet Density Foods is a company in Morgantown, WV dedicated to making nutrient dense foods. I was initially hired to retouch on their original branding and after being happy with the result, I designed the identity for their granola snack, Gravity Bites. The apple logo refers to Isaac Newton, and the Laws of Motion and the kayaker on the label gave a sense of adventure and spirit.
