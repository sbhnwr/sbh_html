Oh hello

I’m Sabah Nawar Ahmed, a designer and illustrator with four years of experience across many mediums like branding, print, UI, and web (just to name a few). When it comes to great design, I believe personality, meaning, and versatility can turn a good project into a memorable experience.

In my spare time, I illustrate and explore themes of identity, materialism, and mental health in the digital age. Sometimes I post them on instagram.

Likes comedy, mangoes, Bob's Burgers, Mario Kart, sarees, and Chardonnay.
